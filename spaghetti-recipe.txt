Ingredients:
- 1/2 box spaghetti noodles
- 1 can spaghetti sauce
- 3 sticks celery
- 6 roma tomatoes
- 1 medium yellow onion
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 lb mild italian sausage

Add salt to 1/2 gallon water and boil noodles until al dente.  Drain noodles when done.

Brown ground beef and sausage in skillet and drain off extra grease. Chop
celery, onion, and tomatoes into bite- sized pieces. Add sauce, celery, and
onion to skillet and simmer 15 minutes.  Add tomatoes and simmer until celery
and onion are tender.

When both noodles and sauce are done, cover noodles in sauce and serve.
